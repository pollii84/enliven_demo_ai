The challenge
Pharmacy Challenges
Pharmacy chains are facing rising call volumes, staffing shortages, and an increasingly fragmented vendor landscape.

Rising call volumes

Pharmacy chains are facing rising call volumes, staffing shortages, and an increasingly fragmented vendor landscape. The Conversational Engagement Platform replaces legacy IVR systems, point SMS tools, and standalone chat solutions with a single AI-powered conversational layer across voice and text—embedded directly into the enterprise patient communications platform. The result: fewer vendors, lower cost, and faster innovation.

Staffing shortages

Every minute spent answering routine calls is a minute away from clinical consultations, dispensing, and patient safety.

Vendor fragmentation

Separate IVR, SMS, and call deflection systems create data silos, inconsistent patient experiences, and inflated costs.

Key capabilities
What the Platform Does
A unified conversational layer across voice and SMS — powered by AI that understands pharmacy.
Voice

Conversational IVR

Natural voice interaction with personalized greetings, intelligent intent detection, mispronunciation handling, and seamless warm transfer to staff with full patient context.

Messaging

Smart SMS

Free-text replies to existing notifications like Rx Ready and refill reminders. AI responds in seconds with conditional authentication — no keywords, no menus.

Caregivers

Family management

Caregivers manage prescriptions for multiple family members in a single call or text session, with per-person HIPAA-compliant re-verification.

Operations

Real-time analytics

Dashboard with containment rates, escalation tracking, cost-per-interaction, staff time saved, and automated alerting when performance degrades.
